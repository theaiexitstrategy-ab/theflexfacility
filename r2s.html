The Training Split

6 Days. Every Muscle. No Excuses.

Each day targets a specific muscle group with 8 movements (4 supersets), progressively overloaded across 4 sets.

1

Day One

Back

Pull-ups, cable rows, barbell rows, single-arm dumbbell rows, rear flys & face pulls — 8 movements, 4 supersets.

2

Day Two

Chest

Push-ups, cable flys, flat bench, incline press, hex press, decline press, Hammer Strength & landmine press.

3

Day Three

Legs

Leg press, squats, leg curls, RDLs, extensions, Bulgarian split squats, reverse lunges & step-ups.

4

Day Four

Shoulders

Lateral raises, dumbbell press, front raises, cable press, high pulls, barbell shrugs & Y-raises.

5

Day Five

Arms

EZ bar curls, dips, alt. dumbbell curls, tricep pushdowns, hammer curls, overhead extensions & diamond push-ups.

6

Day Six

HIIT

Kettlebell swings, mountain climbers, thrusters, burpees, plank jacks, jump jacks, squat jumps & Russian twists.
